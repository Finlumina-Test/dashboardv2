Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Vox

How does Vox handle multiple languages? +
Vox uses OpenAI's Realtime API which supports fluent conversations in English, Urdu, Hindi, Arabic, and 50+ other languages. It automatically detects the caller's language and responds naturally. The AI understands various accents and dialects without any additional setup.
Can I use my existing phone number? +
Yes! You can forward your existing number to Vox or we can provide you with new local numbers in Pakistan, US, UK, or any of 50+ countries. Number porting is also available for businesses that want to fully migrate. Setup takes less than 5 minutes.
What happens if the AI doesn't understand? +
You can take over any call instantly from the dashboard with less than 100ms delay. The AI can also be configured to automatically transfer complex queries to human agents. All calls are recorded and transcribed, so you can review any conversation and improve the AI's responses over time.
Is my data secure? +
Absolutely. Vox is built on SOC 2 certified infrastructure and uses bank-level encryption. All call data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We're GDPR-ready and comply with international data protection regulations. Your data is never used to train AI models. You maintain full ownership and can export or delete data anytime.
How accurate is the order extraction? +
Our AI achieves 95%+ accuracy on order extraction for restaurants and e-commerce. It understands menu items, quantities, delivery addresses, and special requests. Orders are automatically structured and can be sent to your POS system, kitchen display, or order management software via API or webhook.
What's the call quality like? +
Vox uses Twilio's carrier-grade infrastructure with less than 500ms latency. The AI voice sounds natural and conversational, not robotic. We support HD voice where available. The system automatically handles background noise, cross-talk, and poor connections better than traditional IVR systems.
Can I customize the AI's personality? +
Yes! You can customize the AI's tone, style, greetings, and responses. Make it formal for professional services or casual for restaurants. You can also upload your menu, FAQs, and business policies so the AI gives accurate information specific to your business.
How quickly can I get started? +
Most businesses are live within 24 hours. The process: 1) Contact sales, 2) Get a local phone number or forward your existing one, 3) Customize the AI's responses for your business, 4) Go live. Our team helps with setup and training is included.
